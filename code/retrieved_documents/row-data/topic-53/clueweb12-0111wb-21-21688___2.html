    Kyocera FK White Ceramic 11cm Utility Knife

    The Kyocera High Tech Ceramic FK Knives Series features elegant design coupled with outstanding sharpness. The black ergonomic handle is both durable and moisture resistant. Robust white blades will hold their edges much longer than steel. Completely stain and rust proof, the ceramic blades are impervious to food acids that commonly discolour steel and will not impart any metallic taste or smell. Their use will also not brown fruits or vegetables. Extremely easy to clean non-stick ceramic blades are a joy to use - simply wash and dry by hand. These knives are lightweight, perfectly balanced and are second only to diamonds in terms of hardness. This gives a supremely hard wearing, astonishingly sharp cutting edge. Care must be taken not to cut into hard materials such as bones, as this may damage the thin edge of the blade blade.
